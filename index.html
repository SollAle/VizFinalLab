Worlds 2025 — League of Legends Esports Meta Analysis

Project Story & Key Questions

This project explores the competitive meta and team performance trends at the League of Legends World Championship 2025. Using professional match data from Oracle’s Elixir, the dashboard investigates how champion selection, pick/ban priority, and early-game performance relate to match outcomes.

Key Questions

  • Which champions dominate the pick/ban meta?
  • Does high pick priority correlate with higher win rates?
  • How important is early-game advantage in winning matches?
  • Do teams from different regions exhibit distinct performance patterns?

How to Use This Dashboard

  • Navigate to Champion Meta to explore pick, ban, and win-rate relationships.
  • Navigate to Team Performance to analyze regional strength, aggression, and objective control.
  • Use hover interactions and legends to isolate roles, regions, and teams.

Together, these visualizations tell a story about how strategic priorities, early advantages, and regional playstyles shape success on the world stage.
